# 7. Open Problems

Several theoretical questions arise.

## PET Shape Growth

Prove rigorously that

\[
S(N) = \Theta((\log N)^2).
\]

## Generator Sequence

Determine precise asymptotics of \(n_k\).

## Dominant Diagonal

Understand whether the generator sequence corresponds to a minimal path in PET space under the metric

\[
d(n)=\log n.
\]

## Combinatorial Characterization

Develop generating functions describing PET shapes as a constrained family of rooted trees.

## Statistical Properties

Study distributions of:

- width (number of primes),
- depth of exponent trees,
- branching patterns.

Understanding these properties may reveal deeper structural laws governing multiplicative complexity.
